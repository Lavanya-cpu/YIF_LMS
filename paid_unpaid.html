Get Ready To Study!!

YIF(Youth India Foudation) developed this LMS site for providing a special offer for 10th students to help them to
study and prepare for the exams. It has two category offers one is for free and another is for paid thing. In free
section student will get question papers of four subjects(English, Mathematics, Social science and Science) and in paid
section student will be provided with PDF of Books, course materials, reference books, practice quizzes and Recorded vedio
lectures for 10th students.
